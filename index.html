About KM Security

Military veteran's enterprise delivering dependable, compliant and high-quality security solutions

M/S Kanwar Manmeet Security Agency (KM Security) is a military veteran's enterprise headed by Colonel Kanwar Manmeet. KM Security is a Director General of Resettlement empanelled agency having a valid PASARA License and delivers dependable, compliant and high-quality security solutions to public and private establishments.

Our Leadership

Colonel Kanwar Manmeet — Founder and CEO

A distinguished military veteran who has held key Command, Staff and Instructional appointments and has vast experience in the field of security and administration.

His leadership style is rooted in discipline, integrity and accountability – values that define the culture and performance of KM Security.

Our Team

A robust team of trained military veterans and administrative staff ensures seamless operations and effective security deployment.

The team has a military style chain of command to ensure effective supervision, discipline and recruitment.

Our People

All guards are carefully selected Military/CAPF veterans, or experienced, verified civilians, as per client requirement. Every guard undergoes a rigorous verification process followed by a structured security training programme.

Our Approach

At KM Security, leadership is hands-on. Colonel Kanwar Manmeet personally oversees key operational areas to ensure:

  • Superior Service Delivery
  • Rapid Response
  • Client Satisfaction at Every Level
